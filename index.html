Frequently Asked Questions

I just landed at the airport, where do I meet my chauffeur?

Your driver will be waiting at the designated pre-arranged loading zone with your name displayed on a professional sign. Your confirmation email will include specific pickup instructions and your driver's contact information.

What if my flight gets canceled or delayed?

We monitor all flights in real-time. If your flight is delayed, we automatically adjust your pickup time at no additional charge. If your flight is canceled, contact us immediately at (808) 304-8521, and we'll reschedule your service.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations 48+ hours in advance receive a full refund. Cancellations 24-48 hours in advance receive a 50% refund or full credit. Cancellations less than 24 hours receive no refund, though credits may be issued for emergencies.

Do you offer lei greetings?

No, we do not offer lei greetings. However, we do offer orchid leis at $15 each.
